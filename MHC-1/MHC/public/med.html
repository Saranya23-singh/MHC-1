Benefits of Yoga

Yoga helps with anxiety relief by combining physical movement, breath control, and mindfulness. Here's how it works:

  • Activates the Parasympathetic Nervous System
  • Reduces Stress Hormones
  • Encourages Mindfulness & Present-Moment Awareness
  • Releases Muscle Tension
  • Boosts Mood & Emotional Resilience
  • Encourages Self-Compassion & Acceptance
